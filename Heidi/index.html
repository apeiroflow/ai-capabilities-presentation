The Why

Why I Am Drawn to Heidi

My partner is a dental clinician. After every shift, she would spend up to 90 minutes finishing her notes, not because she was slow, but because the tools weren’t built for how clinicians actually work. Every note starts from a blank scratchpad. No templates, no transcription, no patient history in context, no prompts for what needs to be covered. Just memory, and time.

That time came either at the clinic or at home, before any family time. It was the difference between being present or being drained.

In 2023, I built a small clinical documentation tool for her using AI. She used it and we got that time back.

That’s when it clicked. This isn’t just a tooling problem, it’s time, energy, and care. I saw the impact for one clinician. Heidi is doing this at scale.

That’s why I’m here.

Case Study 2

Simple Super

AI-Powered Investment Platform · Mock name, client owns · The same commercial questions required to scale

01 · The Thesis

Trading platform built for a world where AI agents execute on behalf of users. Blockchain infrastructure makes this viable: on-chain data, verifiable reasoning, and execution costs at 10% of the cheapest retail product.

02 · The Strategic Question

Which AI capabilities to build, and at what cost? Mapped the vectors: chatbot, compliance engine, guidance, and advisory. Each carries a different cost structure, monetisation logic, and regulatory implication (robo-adviser licence, FSC).

03 · The Framework

Evaluated every AI vector against cost-to-serve, value capture (CAC, marketing, direct monetisation), and regulatory requirements. Designed a phased approach: build all functionality, then assess what the market values and what regulation permits.

04 · The Architecture

Tokenised assets expand the investable universe. On-chain data is transparent and agent-readable by design. Lower execution costs enable usage volumes that traditional infrastructure cannot serve.
